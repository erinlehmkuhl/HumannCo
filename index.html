ENGINEERING & SURVEYING

The Humann Company began as a small firm, providing services for modest projects to both homeowners and developers alike. Such undertakings included simple boundary surveys for property line fences, additions and landscape improvements, to the design of major subdivisions, grading and drainage plans for single family homes, and the processing of various land use permits. As the corporation expanded, it began to serve large scale projects, but maintained loyalty to the sector that gave the company its start. To this day, The Humann Company is known for its ability to serve clients with projects varying in size scope and budget. This flexibility has contributed to the staying power of the firm as it has weathered many turbulent economic times over the last four decades.
